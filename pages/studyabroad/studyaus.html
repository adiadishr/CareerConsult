Study in Australia

Australia is a well liked destination for international students for further studies.Below are some reasons why one should pursuit australia for their abroad studies -

Easy to Adapt
Australia is a highly favorable study destination for Nepali students due to its safe and secure environment, modern infrastructure, and excellent healthcare services. The country's friendly culture facilitates easy integration for Nepali students, offering peace of mind and a high standard of living.

English Language Proficiency
Studying in Australia provides an opportunity for students to improve their English language skills. English is the primary language of instruction, and students can immerse themselves in an English-speaking environment.

Safe Environment
Australia is known for being a safe and secure destination for international students. The country has strict safety regulations and a low crime rate.

Innovative and Dynamic Economy
Australia has a powerfull and dynamic economy with sectors such as technology, healthcare, and finance experiencing significant growth. This provides opportunities for employment and career advancement for graduates.

General Information
Spoken Language English
Cost of Study Undergraduate- AUD 20,000 - AUD 45,000 per year, Post-graduate- AUD 22,000 - AUD 50,000 per year.
Funding Sources Scholarships, personal savings, part-time work, educational loans, and sponsorships
Exams Required IELTS, or PTE
Degrees Undergraduate, post-graduate, Doctoral and Vocational
Intakes February and July intake
Visa Student Visa Subclass 500
